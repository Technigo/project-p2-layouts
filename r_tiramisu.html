Tiramisu

Sophisticated and classy!

  • 100 g icing sugar
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 2 egg whites
  • 2 cl Amaretto
  • 2 cups strong coffee or espresso
  • 500 g mascarpone
  • 250 g ladyfingers
  • cacao powder
  • Active: 30 mins
  • Resting: 1 h
  • Total: 1 h 30 mins
  • No baking!
  • Stir egg yolks, powdered sugar and Amaretto until creamy.
  • Carefully fold in the mascarpone.
  • Beat the egg whites until stiff and fold in carefully.
  • Soak the lady fingers with the espresso/coffee or just brush them according to taste.
  • Create 4 layers: Ladyfingers, cream mixture, ladyfingers, cream mixture.
  • Thickly dust with cocoa and keep cool.
  • Enjoy!
  • If you stir mascarpone too much or mix it with a blender, it might become liquid. Therefore carefully fold the mascarpone into the egg yolk mixture with a mixing spoon or whisk and also fold in the egg whites and do not stir too vigorously.
  • Enjoy a tasty Amaretto Sour cocktail while the dessert is cooling in the fridge :)
