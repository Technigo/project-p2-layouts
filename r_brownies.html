Fudgy Brownies

You can never go wrong with brownies!

  • 195 g flour
  • 1/2 tbsp salt
  • 225 g butter, in cubes
  • 450 g sugar
  • 60 ml espresso
  • 300 g dark chocolate
  • 60 g chocolate, unsweetened
  • 1 package vanilla sugar
  • 4 big eggs
  • 2 cups chopped walnuts
  • Active: 30 mins
  • Baking: 20 mins
  • Total: 50 mins
  • Preheat oven to 180Â°C
  • Bake 20-25 mins
  • Butter a rectangular baking pan (approx. 22x33cm)
  • Heat butter, sugar, vanilla sugar and coffee under medium heat, stirring constantly.
  • Take mixture off heat, add chocolate and keep stirring until the chocolate has melted completely. Let cool.
  • Add eggs carfully to chocolate mixture and mix well.
  • Mix flour and salt and add to the rest until well blended. Add walnuts.
  • Add dough to baking form and bake 20-25 minutes. Let cool and cut into squares.
  • Enjoy!
  • The brownies should still be moist after baking.
  • Serve warm and with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.
