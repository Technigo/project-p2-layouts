Apple Crumble

As easy as it gets!

  • 5 big apples
  • 90 g brown sugar
  • 150 g flour
  • 90g butter
  • some cinnamon
  • Active: 20 mins
  • Baking: 30 mins
  • Total: 50 mins
  • Preheat oven to 200Â°C
  • Bake 30 mins
  • Peel the apples, cut into small pieces and add into baking pan.
  • Cut butter into very small cubes. Add flour, sugar and cinnamon and manually mix to create the crumble.
  • Sprinkle crumble over the apple and bake for 30 minutes.
  • Enjoy!
  • Serve warm with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream
