Tarte au Chocolat

Chocolate, butter, sugar - the base ingredients for a deliciously guilty pleasure!

  • 200 g butter
  • 200 g chocolate (milk oder dark, as you like)
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 package vanilla sugar
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 100 g chocolate, chopped or chocolate chips
  • 2 tbsp corn starch + 2 tbsp cacao powder
  • 1/2 Package baking powder
  • butter for the form
  • chocolate icing (optional)
  • Active: 15 mins
  • Baking: 30 mins
  • Total: 45 mins
  • Preheat to 175°C
  • Bake 20-25 mins
  • Preheat oven to 175°C.
  • Melt butter and chocolate over hot water. Take off heat and let cool.
  • Mix eggs, vanilla sugar, salt and sugar together and beat until creamy and light.
  • Carefully and slowly add chocolate-butter-mix and the chopped chocolate and fold together.
  • Mix cacao powder, corn starch and baking powder and add to the mixture.
  • Pour into a buttered spring form (26cm) and bake for 20-25 minutes.
  • Remove from oven and let it cool and settle.
  • Coat with chocolate icing and/or cocoa powder
  • Enjoy!
  • The cake should be still moist after baking
  • Optional: substitute the chopped chocolate with chopped nuts of your choice
  • The higher the cacao-% of the chocolate, the more sugar is recommended to avoid bitterness. For example, with 85% chocolate, we recommend to use 130-150 g of sugar
