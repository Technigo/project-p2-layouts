Coconut Panna Cotta

Italian Classic with a Tropical Spin!

  • 350 ml heavy cream
  • 150 ml coconut milk
  • 50 g sugar
  • 1 vanilla bean
  • 2 sticks lemon grass
  • 3 sheets gelatine
  • 4 passion fruits
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 4 organic lemons, juice and zest
  • 1 vanilla bean
  • Active: 20 mins
  • Resting: 4 h
  • Total: 4 h 20 mins
  • No baking involved!
  • Mix heavy cream and coconut milk in a pot. Scrape out the vanilla pod and finely chop the lemon grass.
  • Add the vanilla, lemon grass and sugar to the cream mixture and briefly bring to the boil.
  • Remove from heat, cover and leave to stand for about 2 hours.
  • Then bring to the boil again, soak the gelatine in cold water and dissolve in the cream.
  • Pass the mixture through a sieve and put it into small bowls or glasses. Let it set in the refrigerator.
  • Cut the passionfruit in half, scrape out the flesh and put it into a pot. Bring to the boil with honey, lemon juice, finely grated lemon peel and the scraped out vanilla pod and let it simmer for about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool.
  • Pour the cold fruit puree onto the panna cotta and serve.
  • Enjoy!
  • This amount is enough for 8 small glasses of 80 ml.
  • Both the panna cotta and the fruit puree can be prepared a day before.
