Making Life Sweeter One Bite at a Time

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Happiness served one bite at a time!

  • 2 eggs
  • 130 g white sugar
  • 150 g brown sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking poweder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 200 g margarine or butter, softened
  • 380 g flour
  • 200 g chocolate chips
  • Active: 20 mins
  • Baking: 15 mins
  • Total: 35 mins
  • Preheat oven to 150Â°C
  • Bake 15 mins
  • Mix the ingredients in the order given above.
  • The mixture should be a bit dry at first and the butter should be soft enough to be mixed in smoothly.
  • Spread 9 dough balls in rows of three on two baking trays covered with baking paper.
  • The balls will melt into to the round cookie form wile baking, no need to press them down or form them.
  • Bake one tray at a time on the middle rail with bottom heat (circulating air not recommended) for about 15 minutes.
  • Take out of the oven and let cool.
  • Enjoy!
  • The cookies will still be quite pale when coming out of the oven, but that must be the case, otherwise they will not stay soft.
  • Press a little bit on the top of a cookie with a fork. If the surface gives in slightly without cracking they are good to go!
  • Serve with a glass of milk.
