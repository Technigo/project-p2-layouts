Chocolate Lava Cake

Guaranteed to melt even the coldest hearts!

  • 210 g butter
  • 210 g chocolate
  • 7 eggs
  • 270 g sugar
  • 120 g flour
  • 40 g cocoa powder
  • Active: 20 mins
  • Baking: 7 mins
  • Total: 27 mins
  • Preheat oven to 210Â°C
  • Bake 7 mins
  • Melt butter and chocolate over hot water. Take off heat and let cool.
  • Mix with eggs and sugar and blend together.
  • Add flour and cacao powder and mix until smooth.
  • Pour into small greased baking molds (ca. 7.5 cm diameter, 4 cm high) and bake 7 to max. 10 minutes.
  • Enjoy!
  • The cake will be liquid inside.
  • Serve with fresh fruit or vanilla ice cream.
